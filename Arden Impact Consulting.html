Who We Serve

Partners in complex, mission-driven healthcare work

Arden Impact Consulting partners with healthcare organizations navigating complex grants and operational demands. Our clients often manage multiple funding sources, regulatory requirements, and cross-functional teams — and need experienced support to keep everything aligned, efficient, and compliant.

We work with organizations that are growing, restructuring, or managing complex grant portfolios and need clear systems, reliable processes, and experienced guidance. Our approach is practical: organizing what exists, addressing gaps, and establishing structures that can be maintained by internal teams.

Clients engage Arden Impact when they need senior-level expertise without adding permanent staff — and when clarity, compliance, and continuity are essential.

Our Founder

Two decades at the intersection of healthcare and public health

Maria Espinal is a healthcare executive with more than two decades of experience leading grants management, financial oversight, and operational initiatives within academic medical centers — including New York Presbyterian Hospital and New York University School of Medicine — with a sustained focus on community and population health in complex, highly regulated environments.

She holds a Master of Public Health from the CUNY School of Public Health, a Bachelor of Science in Healthcare Administration from New York University, and is an AMA-Certified Professional in Management. She was named a 2024 Dalio Health Justice REACH Fellow at Columbia University Mailman School of Public Health.
